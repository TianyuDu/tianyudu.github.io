What's This?

Since my first year at UofT, I have been typesetting lecture notes for core courses (mainly econ and math courses) to keep things organized. Hopefully, these materials are helpful. Contents covered in each course vary often, please refer to your syllabus as well while preparing for exams. Please email me at tianyudu@stanford.edu if you found typos, I would truly appreciate it!

Latex source codes of these notes are publicly available on github.
